What is Health & Wellness?

The words health and wellbeing are often mistakenly used interchangeably, however they are some key differences between the two. For health, the physical, mental and social attributes of an individual is an indicator of their health status, whilst wellness relates to an individual achieving their optimal state of health Another point of differentiation is that wellness is an ongoing and active process where an individual continuously makes choices in their everyday life that will help them to reach their peak health.

As you may see, health and wellness have areas of overlap, i.e. physical, mental, emotional etc., such that when focus is placed on training one area, it could improve the other areas synergically. For example, daily excercise can benefit your physical health and improve your mental and emotional health at the same time, or a living space that is neat and clean can provide great environment wellness and have positive effect on your physical, mental, emotional and spiritual health too.

Why is it important?

Life demands a certain level of resilience since it is full of potential challenges at every single moment. Maintaining good health and wellness is a great way to build your defense against these challenges. By doing so, you would gain higher productivity in your personal life and work. Furthermore, you can gain more control and understanding of your own abilities to better realise your own potential, and ultimately become a better contributor to the community around you and society in general.

Who we are, and why we want to help

The UQ Wellness Ambassadors Network is a university-wide network, with representatives from across different schools and institutes. Representatives from SCMB have come together to create a new initative that aims to help the students and staff within the school in looking after their health and wellbeing.

How

We will provide you with tips, tricks, resources, and let you know about upcoming events and activities that promote your health and wellbeing, such as R U OK? Day, Womens Health Week, Mens Health Week, Carers Week etc.

Your journey towards better health and wellness can start now by following some of the tips below.

Tips to achieve general health and wellness

- Practice gratitude and mindfulness
- Eat well
- Get quality sleep
- Exercise - go for a walk during your break, cycle to work, etc.
- Rest when needed
- Surround yourself with positive and supportive people
